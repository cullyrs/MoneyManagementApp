About Expense Tracker

Expense Tracker is a powerful yet easy-to-use tool that helps individuals take control of their personal finances. It provides users with the ability to monitor their income and expenses, set financial goals, and gain valuable insights into their spending habits. With advanced features like exporting data to CSV and generating monthly summaries, Expense Tracker makes financial management simple and accessible for everyone.

Features

Expense Tracker is packed with a wide range of features tailored to suit your financial needs. Users can create and customize categories for tracking specific expenses, enabling detailed monitoring of their financial activities. The application encourages better saving habits by allowing users to set financial goals and track their progress. Secure login functionality ensures the safety of your data, while real-time updates on the financial dashboard provide immediate insights into your finances.
